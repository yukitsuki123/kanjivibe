# Kanjivibe: Liquid Glass Darknight Design Tokens & Animation States ## Design Tokens ### Colors - **Background (Midnight Blue):** `#050A18` - **Glass Card:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` with `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` - **Glass Border:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.15)` (simulating light hitting glass) - **Primary Accent (Electric Purple):** `#7C3AED` - **Secondary Accent (Cyan):** `#06B6D4` - **Text (High Emphasis):** `#F8FAFC` - **Text (Low Emphasis):** `#94A3B8` ### Typography - **Font Family:** 'Space Grotesk' or similar modern sans-serif. - **Kanji Display:** Large, bold weight with slight outer glow for emphasis. ### Layout - **Bento Grid:** Standard 16px gutter between modules. - **Roundness:** 24px (Large) for cards and blobs to maintain an organic feel. --- ## Animation States & Liquid Transitions ### 1. Liquid Swipe (Flashcards) - **State:** Resting -> Dragging -> Released (Dismiss/Next) - **Transition:** As the user drags the card, the edges should slightly distort or "stretch" in the direction of the drag, mimicking a viscous fluid. On release, the card "snaps" or "splashes" out of view with a spring-based velocity. ### 2. Morphing Blobs (Module Selection) - **State:** Idle (Floating) -> Hover/Selected (Morphing) - **Transition:** Categories are contained within SVG blobs. When selected, the blob uses a `pop` and `morph` animation to change its vertex positions smoothly, transitioning from one organic shape to another while changing the accent glow intensity. ### 3. Glass Entrance - **State:** Hidden -> Visible - **Transition:** Components fade in with a slight vertical offset (Y: 20 -> 0) while the blur radius increases from 0 to 20px, creating a "condensing" effect on the screen.
